Wingtip Boots

Men’s wingtip boots have come a long way since their humble beginning as an all-weather, all-purpose shoe. The now instantly-recognizable brogue design that features distinct serrating and perforation was at one point implemented as a means of drainage so as to increase the boot’s utility in wet terrain. Always regarded for their sturdiness and reliability, men’s wingtip boots have remained a favorite throughout the centuries due to their distinct look and excellent functionality. This selection maintains the rich historicity of the wingtip footwear while including more modern sensibilities: adjustable pin-buckle straps feature impressive hardware, signature detailing and embroidery create truly memorable silhouettes, while zip closures ensure comfort and ease of wear. Although previously looked down upon from the menswear sphere of influence, the wingtip boot now represents an audacious option compared to their more circumspect lace-up brethren when dressing for formal occasions. Iconic patterned detailing makes for a memorable and masterfully crafted boot wherein Old World charm meets New World inventiveness.
