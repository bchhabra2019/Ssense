Suits & Blazers

Diverse visions of modern menswear take shape in sharp suiting that holds the ability to befit casual and formal dress codes alike. Thoughtfully tailored designer men's suits and blazers crafted in an assortment of rich fabrics give sartorial refinement an everyday ease; everybody looks better wearing a suit. Patterned jackets favor the bold while featuring modern checks, slim cuts, and lean lapels that mix well with lightweight underpinnings. Elsewhere, an undeniable individuality is achieved through raw-edged finishes and offbeat hardware, while contrast stitching provides a playful departure for more subtle pieces.
