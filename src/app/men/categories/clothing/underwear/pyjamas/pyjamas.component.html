Pyjamas & Loungewear

Echoing both the transformations and enduring tropes of modern masculinity, contemporary designer underwear and loungewear cuts a dynamic range of forms. Low to mid-rise boxer briefs are tailored in a range of premium sourced fabrics, offering essential freedom of movement and comfort for ideal fit with a range of trousers, jeans, and shorts. Boxer shorts add new designs or careful updating to a classic silhouette, as the pragmatic design, devised and intended to grant freedom of movement to literal boxers, maintains its continued innerwear relevancy. Intarsia knit logo patterning at waistband, a signature of select houses, hints at the optics of peek display, and the pleasures of hidden symbolism. Choice of colors, or lack thereof, express personal preference in an endlessly fascinating way. Bright floral, geometric, and graphic prints offer innerwear expression running from the mild to the eccentric. Stripes and tonal detailing offer subdued accents of a more minimal variety.
