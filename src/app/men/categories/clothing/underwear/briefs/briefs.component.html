Briefs

The unparalleled support and sleekness offered by men’s briefs is accentuated in this latest variety of designer interpretations. A divisive yet enduringly popular option in the gamut of men’s underwear, briefs are a go-to for the minimalist in pursuit of a straightforward and non-intrusive undermost layer. Breathable and agile stretch jersey constructions are suited to active lifestyles, while explorations in satiny textures add a dimension of sophistication to an often overlooked staple of everyday wear. An element of playfulness is injected via unexpectedly vibrant graphic prints and self-reflexive slogans, while committedly simple multipack variations offered in muted colorways instill an unfussy sense of uniformity into undergarment selection. Brand iconography is either woven effortlessly into elasticized waistbands or translated into bold all-over prints, suggesting that the brief’s status as an ultimately concealed item of clothing affords an endless range of audacious design opportunities. In this curated repertoire of mens briefs, tradition and experimentation coalesce to reaffirm of the reasons for which high-end underwear are equal parts indulgent luxury and indispensable necessity.
