Robes

Epitomizing the meeting point between luxury and lethargy, the robe is a pivotal yet often overlooked piece of off-duty essential wear. The robe carries with it a spirit of pampering and relaxation that has made it a mainstay of hotels and spas alike, and its silk variations have become irrevocably tied to a caricatural portrait of the bon-vivant. Thriving on the novelty appeal of this unabashedly posh loungewear staple, designer robes augment luxe terrycloth constructions with their glaring trademarks, doubling down on the dandy aesthetic that the garment inherently connotes. Flannel variations push the robe beyond post-shower or post-swim contexts, resulting in a hybrid between domestic lazy Sunday garb and off-kilter outerwear. The recent embrace of high-end commodities has inevitably altered the level of attention paid to the formerly neglected realm of casual apparel, a category which the robe has thus far belonged to.
