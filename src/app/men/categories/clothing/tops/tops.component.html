Tops

Highlighting values that include, but are not limited to, those of pure expressionism as well as a sense of elegantly refined minimalism, designer men's tops are essential to any modern wardrobe rotation. Men's t-shirts that are crafted in premium and organic cottons remain quintessential casualwear staples, but receive avant-garde updates by way of overlong silhouettes, layered designs, and panelled constructions. Elsewhere, cotton piqué polo shirts brandish embroidered logos with contrasting plackets and preppy stripes that aim to complement low-key tailoring expertly. For unexpected dimension look no further than embroidered leather appliqués and graphic patches. And for those who understand the value of an attention-grabbing visual stimulation: vivid colorblocking and bold all-over prints confidently complement more boisterous proclivities.
