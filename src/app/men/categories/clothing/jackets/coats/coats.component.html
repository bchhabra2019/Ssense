Coats

Streamlined tailoring and premium textiles merge in this season’s varied selection of coats for men. Ranging from casual to formal, this season’s selection of coats offers tasteful and functional options to complete any ensemble. Classic form meets avant-garde proportions updated with modern detailing like velvet or lambskin trim, graphic patchwork, beaded appliqués, and colorful jacquard patterning. Peaked lapel collars exemplify a sophisticated expression of eveningwear, while a simple stand or notched lapel collar adapt to any outfit pairing. Advancements in technology are reflected in luxe faux-fur or faux-shearling textures, integrated interior shoulder straps, or technical bonded detailing. Trans-seasonal belted trench coats, tailored raincoats, and double-breasted overcoats remain go-to staples for minimal outerwear styling. Oversized herringbone or houndstooth patterned coats showcase dropped shoulders and neat lapels for simple, yet protective coverage. While streetwear offerings of elongated padded nylon coats offer a refined approach to maximizing warmth. Mohair and silk-blends, brushed wool, twills, and tweeds are all a part of the exceptional textile quality that make up this season’s collection of menswear coats.
