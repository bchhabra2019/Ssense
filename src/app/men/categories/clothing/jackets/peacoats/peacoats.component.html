Peacoats

As the archetypal maritime menswear staple of masculinity, the peacoat offers both a formidable sense of sophistication and prowess that can be paired with most any option whether formal or casual. The classic double-breasted garment retains much of its traditional militaristic charm, but this season’s selection proves the fluidity inherent among nautical goods. Oversized options featuring cropped hems, detachable collars, or dropped shoulders define an avant-garde silhouette that pay homage to elements of high fashion. Whereas integrated designs featuring signature detailing re-historicize the garment. And yet, the peacoat’s storied history as an irreplaceable naval symbol clearly isn’t for naught as this garment’s functionality are still easily testified. Luxurious wool fabrics assure coziness throughout a range of seasons, announcing a refined style as well as issuing a challenge to the elements. Flipped-collar convertibility guarantees not only increased warmth but a new channel for styling. A luxurious design that is exceedingly easy to wear, this men’s coat can be dressed up or down with little cause for stress.
