Fur & Shearling

An instant signifier of opulence, men’s fur and shearling coats elevate the pedigree of any outfit whether formal or casual. This season’s selection of naturally-insulated menswear garments exceeds expectations for cold weather-wear with its broad range of avant-garde fur-trimmed collars and refined traditional designs. Updated military classics such as the shearling-lined B-3 bomber jacket soar, while boldly colorblocked reversible fur jackets embody an urban-centric sensibility. Modern takes on classically cut trucker jackets feature colorful embroidery to new depth to the historically indomitable style. Fur-lined hooded parkas, both long and short, come in a bountiful selection of colors, fabrics, and hems to satisfy any requisite need. Designers bring back signature styles to create truly recognizable masculine silhouettes. Intimate detailing, clean tailoring, and luxurious fabrications ensure that these coats not only embody the zeitgeist of modern luxury, but undoubtedly retain warmth in even the coldest of climates.
