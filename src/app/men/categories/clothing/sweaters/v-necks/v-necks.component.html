V-Necks

A classic menswear fixture, the V-neck offers a clean, geometric silhouette that cuts across casual ready-to-wear and formal contexts. The contemporary fashion forecast for the V-neck sweater is indeed more promising than ever, a reality evidenced by the stapleâ€™s full-hearted re-embrace by the seminal houses of the luxury brand landscape. Oversized, angular v-neck sweaters sit on dropped shoulders to effect a suitably of-the-moment deconstructed, slouchy, and post-gender cool. Modified, slit-collar variants nod to avant-garde trends in high fashion towards disrupted shapes and re-animating timeworn staples by allowing them a multiplicity of wearing contexts. Alternate fabrics like garment-pleated synthetics and semi-sheer silk opt towards statement-making, developing a sense of leisurely, self-confident poise. Cotton poplin and jersey shirts often work with the sleek, understated pleasure of perfect ratios, taking minimal accents and embellishments and updating heritage shapes. V-neck sweaters and tops wear well alone, providing a pared-down charm that fits easily with tailored trousers or designer denims. Worn as a layer, V-necks compliment collared, mock neck, or turtleneck shirts in equal measure, stabilizing an ensemble and providing a more constructed look.
