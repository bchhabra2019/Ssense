Travel Bags

Providing ample storage in roomy sizes, this season’s collection of men’s travel bags are a must for weekend getaway road trips and airport-hopping during long-haul sejours. Classic interpretations of the structured carryall include boxy constructions in textural compositions of canvas, grained leather, and coated nylon. Maintaining a clean-lined minimalist aesthetic, the bulk of this collection of travel bags for men features a solid achromatic palette. Select labels offer up printed graphic patterns in natural or muted earth-tones, while playful graphic appliqués provide instant street appeal. Not just for the average commuter, this season’s selection of highly-versatile men’s travel bags expands its definition of ‘travel’ to bring a taste of modern urban design to even the most daring of outdoorsmen, with styles like the crossbody perfect for hiking and mountaineering. Utilitarian details including keyring lanyards, concealed security zip pockets, and convertible shoulder straps make for efficient and organized outings, while hyper-pragmatic accents like collapsible carry handles, multiple zippered compartments, interior elasticized straps, and 360° rotating wheels appeal to the most frequent of flyers.
