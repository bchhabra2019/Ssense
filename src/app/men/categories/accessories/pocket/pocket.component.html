Pocket Squares & Tie Bars

Essential to the realm of men’s formalwear, pocket squares and tie bars easily elevate any tailored suit ensemble. Conceived in the 19th century to accompany the classic men’s two-piece suit, preliminary pocket squares and tie bars served squarely functional purposes. No longer confined to their utilitarian function in recent years, this duo of menswear accessories has become a signifier of sophistication and refinement. This season’s selection of meticulously-crafted luxury pocket squares and tie bars highlights the timeless quality of this pair of formal accoutrements. Modern iterations of the tie bar feature intricate carved detailing, a nod the accessory’s inherent vintage charm, while sterling silver logo-engraved iterations become subtle statement pieces, all while neatly keeping slim neck ties in place. Crafted from luxe silk satins and twills, patterned, striped, and printed pocket squares emerge as expressive accents to streamlined styling, while an assortment of bright hues bring a touch of color to black-tie events. No matter the occasion, designer pocket squares and tie bars bring a fresh outlook to formal dress codes and are the certain to be the perfect companion to any suit-and-tie look.
