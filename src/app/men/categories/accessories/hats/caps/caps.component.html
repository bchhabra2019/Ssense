Caps & Flat Caps

Classic and contemporary caps and flat caps complete most any ensemble, offering men mix-and-match contrast and fluid accents across all seasons. The baseball cap, venerable fashion staple of the past century, has enjoyed a marked renaissance in recent years, with everything from five-panel skatewear caps, flat-brimmed snapbacks, and slouchy low-profile six-panel silhouettes now well and truly in play. The present day selection of high-end designer pieces can convey luxe excess, sturdy formalism, or add a disarmingly laid-back, everyman quality to a couture or classically preppy outfit. Logos, appliqu√©s, and tonal and contrasted text embroidery make visual and rhetorical statements big and small. Materials like suede, wool and linen-blend, leather, cotton twill, and nylon and technical synthetic performance fabrics signify intention, from post-normcore leisure aesthetic to boss-mode opulence or minimalist futurism. Closures further articulate personality and style, taking the form of cinch-straps, sometimes exaggerated and overlong, or Velcro and press-release fastenings. The range of possible configurations provides a foil for virtually every traditional formalwear costume, athleisure uniform, or streetwear juxtaposition.
